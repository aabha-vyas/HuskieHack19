An Overlooked Epidemic

The large prevalence of poor mental health in the American youth inspired us to create this interactive website. 1 in 5 American children between the ages of 13-18 report that they are not mentally well. This is an emergent problem that we feel needs to be resolve in a modern and simple way.

Mental Health. Assesed and Addressed.

Warning signs such as the ones depicted to the side, have helped us to incorporate better questions that would help us to diagnose different conditions. A thorough research has helped us to understand the different types of mental obstacles and incorporate the symptoms/warning in a way that is used to more accurately diagnose the user's condition.
